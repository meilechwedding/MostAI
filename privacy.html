Privacy

Last updated: June 2026

The short version

We only collect what you choose to send us, we use it to talk to you about working together, and we never sell it. That's the whole idea.

What we collect

When you fill in our contact form or email us, we receive the details you provide — typically your name, company, email address, and whatever you tell us about your business. We don't run advertising trackers or sell data to anyone.

Why we collect it

So we can reply to your enquiry, understand what you need, and figure out whether and how we can help. That's it.

How it's handled

Form submissions are delivered to us by email through Web3Forms, a form-delivery service. Once we have your message it lives in our email and notes, treated as a normal business conversation. Standard, off-the-shelf tools (like our website host) may process basic technical data such as your IP address to serve the page.

Your choices

You can ask us at any time to show you what we hold about you, correct it, or delete it. Just email us and we'll take care of it.

Get in touch

Questions about any of this? Email elimelechmoster@gmail.com and we'll be straight with you.
